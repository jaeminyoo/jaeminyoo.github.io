Jaemin Yoo

Ph.D. Candidate at Seoul National University

I am a Ph.D. candidate majoring in Computer Science and Engineering at Seoul National University, advised by Prof. U Kang. I received my B.S. in Computer Science and Engineering from Seoul National University. My research interests include data mining and machine learning. I am a recipient of the Google PhD Fellowship Program in Machine Learning.
